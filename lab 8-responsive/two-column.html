Princess and the Popstar

A Tale of Friendship and Dreams

About the Story

"Princess and the Popstar" is a magical story about two girls from completely different worlds who magically switch places. The princess dreams of living a normal life, while the popstar wishes for freedom from fame and stardom.

Through their adventure, they learn that while the grass may seem greener on the other side, staying true to who you are is the most magical thing of all.
